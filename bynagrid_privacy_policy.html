Privacy Policy

Byna Grid  ·  Effective date: May 2026

Short version: Byna Grid does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Everything stays on your device.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Byna Grid ("the App") handles information. The App is a single-player logic puzzle game that runs entirely on your device without any server connection.

2. Information We Do Not Collect None

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or gameplay statistics
  • Crash reports sent to external servers
  • Any data that leaves your device

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App saves the following data using your device's local storage (Android SharedPreferences). This data never leaves your device:

  • Level completion status and star ratings
  • Best completion times per level
  • Which levels have been unlocked
  • Sound effects preference (on/off)

This data is stored solely to preserve your game progress and preferences between sessions. It is deleted when you uninstall the App.

4. Internet & Network Access

The App does not require an internet connection and does not make any network requests. No data is transmitted to any server at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or social platforms. There are no third parties that receive any information from the App.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. Because we collect no personal information whatsoever, the App fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

7. Your Rights

Because no personal data is collected or stored remotely, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on our end. To remove all locally stored data, simply uninstall the App from your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes in the future, the updated version will be made available through the App's store listing. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the App's privacy practices, you may contact the developer through the Google Play Store listing page for Byna Grid.
